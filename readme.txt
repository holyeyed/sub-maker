This web app help you to make Karaoke subtitle easier.
Usage:
1. Prepare
- A lyric text file, contain all lines of lyric for you music video (.txt)
- A media file of type video or audio (.mp4, .mp3)
2. load your text and media file
3. click getLine to prepare lines data
4. Click Play/pause to start or stop recording
- While your media is playing, press Space keyboard to mark the time of current word of lyric
5. Click "Export" to perform your result.
6. Export to save your ".ass" subtitle.
have fun!
